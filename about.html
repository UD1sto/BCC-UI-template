About Black Card Club NFTs

The Black Card Club is aiming to offer and extend the perks previously available only to premium credit card users to NFT holders. We identified early on the need for utility NFTs in the Customer Rewards Industry. At its current state the traditional web2 market offers plenty of club based reward systems, most of them tightly coupled with strict terms of use that bar customers from legally selling or exchanging their reward points, moreover the reward instruments(credit or club cards) are in most cases tied to an identity, thus depriving the customers from selling their access to rewards to someone else. This of course is not the case with the Black Card Club, we are offering a reward system that the NFT holders can gift, exchange and sell freely both their reward instruments(NFTs) and their accumulated reward points.
